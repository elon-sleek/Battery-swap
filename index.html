PROCESS

From Petrol to Electric
in 3 Steps.

STEP 01

Bring Your Keke

Bring your tuk-tuk to our conversion centre. Our engineers assess it, strip the petrol drivetrain, and prepare it for electrification. Most conversions take 48–72 hours.

We install our purpose-built EV kit: motor, battery housing, smart controller and safety systems. Your keke is then certified and registered as an electric vehicle.
